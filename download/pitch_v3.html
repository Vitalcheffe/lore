02
Label Design
The secret sauce that makes NLI work
7-8 terms per label
"mental health therapy counseling psychological
wellbeing psychiatric emotional support"
— 8 terms, zero overlap with other categories
Too short?
Labels with 1-2 words can't distinguish categories
"health" "help" "support"
→ Which category? Impossible to tell.
Too long?
Labels with 15+ words cause "entailment bleed"
The model matches too broadly,
destroying category boundaries
7-8 terms?
Just right. Accurate classification.
Distinct
Accurate
Reliable
